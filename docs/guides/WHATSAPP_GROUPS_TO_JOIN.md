## 🚀 Step-by-Step Promotion Plan

### Week 1: Join & Observe (Days 1-3)

1. **Join 10-15 groups** from different categories
2. **Observe** group dynamics and rules
3. **Participate naturally** - say hi, respond to messages
4. **Don't promote yet** - build trust first

### Week 1: Soft Introduction (Days 4-7)

1. **Engage in conversations** about games, entertainment
2. **When someone asks** "what games do you play?" → mention your bot
3. **Share naturally** when topic is relevant
4. **Use `.invite` message** when appropriate

### Week 2: Active Promotion

1. **Post `.invite` message** in 5 groups per day
2. **Space out posts** - don't spam
3. **Respond to questions** about the bot
4. **Share success stories** (leaderboard screenshots)

### Week 3-4: Scale Up

1. **Join 20 more groups**
2. **Track which groups** respond best
3. **Focus on active groups**
4. **Create demo videos/screenshots**

## ⚠️ Important Rules to Follow

### DO:

✅ Read group rules before posting

✅ Respond to questions about the bot
✅ Share when topic is relevant
