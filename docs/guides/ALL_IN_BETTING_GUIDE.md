# 🎰 ALL IN BETTING GUIDE

## ✨ New Feature: Bet Everything!

You can now use the keyword `all` to bet your entire balance in gambling games!

---

## 🎮 Supported Games

### 1. Slot Machine

```
.slot all
```

- Spins the slot machine with your entire balance
- Win big multipliers or lose it all!
- Perfect for high-risk, high-reward gameplay

### 2. Dice Game

```
.dice all
```

- Roll dice with everything you have
- Win conditions:
  - Doubles (same number): 3x your bet
  - Total 7 or 11: 2x your bet
  - Other totals: lose your bet

### 3. Coin Flip

```
.coin all heads
.coin all tails
```

- Flip a coin with your entire balance
- Choose heads or tails
- Win: Double your money (2x)
- Lose: Lose everything

---

## 💡 How It Works

1. **Check Your Balance First**

   ```
   .bank
   ```

   See how much you have before going all in!

2. **Use "all" Instead of a Number**

   ```
   Instead of: .slot 100
   Use: .slot all
   ```

3. **The Bot Automatically Bets Everything**
   - Reads your current balance
   - Bets the full amount
   - No need to type the exact number

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Risk Management

- **You can lose everything!** Only use "all" if you're ready to risk it all
- Check your balance with `.bank` before going all in
- Consider the odds of each game before betting everything

### Game Odds

- **Slot Machine**: Hardest to win (5-15% for big wins)
- **Dice**: Medium difficulty (doubles or 7/11)
- **Coin Flip**: 50/50 chance (easiest odds)

### Strategy Tips

1. **Start Small**: Build up your balance first
2. **Know When to Stop**: Don't chase losses
3. **Use Shop Items**: Buy luck boosts before going all in
4. **Coin Flip for Safety**: Best odds if you want to double up

## 🎯 When to Use All In

### Good Times to Go All In:

✅ You have a small balance and want to rebuild quickly
✅ You're feeling lucky and want maximum excitement
✅ You have luck boost items from the shop
✅ You're playing coin flip (50/50 odds)
✅ You're ready to accept the risk

### Bad Times to Go All In:

❌ You just won big and want to keep your winnings
❌ You're on a losing streak

❌ You're playing slot machine (lowest odds)
❌ You're not prepared to lose everything
