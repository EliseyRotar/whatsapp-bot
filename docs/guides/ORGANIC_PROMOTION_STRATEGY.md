## 🎯 The REAL Way to Get Users

### Method 1: Direct Outreach

1. **Find people asking for bots** on Reddit/Discord
2. **Respond helpfully** with your bot
3. **Don't spam** - be genuinely helpful
4. **Build reputation** first

### Method 2: Content Marketing

1. **Write a blog post** about building the bot
2. **Post on Dev.to** or Medium
3. **Share on Hacker News**
4. **Cross-post to Reddit**

### Method 3: Community Building

1. **Create your own Discord server**
2. **Invite initial users** (friends, family)
3. **Let them invite friends** (referral system)
4. **Organic growth** from there

### Method 4: Influencer Outreach

1. **Find micro-influencers** (1K-10K followers)
2. **In gaming/tech niche**
3. **Offer free promotion** in exchange for shoutout
4. **Track results**

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Quality > Quantity**
   - 100 engaged users > 1,000 inactive users
   - Focus on retention first

2. **Be Authentic**
   - Don't pretend to be a user
   - Be honest about being the creator
   - Respond to feedback

3. **Provide Value**
   - Help people first
   - Promote second
   - Build trust

4. **Track Everything**
   - Where users come from
   - What features they use
   - Why they leave

5. **Iterate Fast**
   - Listen to feedback
   - Add requested features
   - Fix bugs quickly
