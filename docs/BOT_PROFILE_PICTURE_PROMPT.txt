⚡ PRO TIPS TO AVOID "AI-GENERATED" LOOK:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Reference real art styles: "in the style of [artist name]" or "like [brand] logo"
2. Use photography terms: "studio lighting", "bokeh", "shallow depth of field"
3. Specify medium: "oil painting", "vector art", "product photography"
4. Add imperfections: "subtle texture", "hand-drawn", "visible brush strokes"
5. Avoid overused AI words: skip "ethereal", "majestic", "intricate details"
6. Be specific about composition: "centered", "rule of thirds", "close-up portrait"
7. Reference real cameras/renders: "shot on Canon 5D", "octane render", "Unreal Engine"

✅ QUICK CHECKLIST:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Before generating:
□ Prompt mentions a specific art style or medium
□ Colors match your brand (purple/cyan)
□ Design will work at small sizes (test at 100x100px)
□ Main subject is centered and clear
□ No text in the image (hard to read when small)
□ Background isn't too busy

After generating:
□ Crop to square if needed
□ Test as WhatsApp profile pic (circular crop)
□ Check visibility in dark mode
□ Resize to 640x640px for WhatsApp (optimal size)
